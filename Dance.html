Dance Club

They say, "Dancers don't need wings to fly". I don't know whether you've heard this or not but ask any member of our dance club and you will get a resounding "True" for an answer. The Dance Club is one of the most glamorous clubs at IIT Goa. The participation in this club is overwhelming and the Group Dance events are among the most eagerly awaited events of the year. It also provides wannabes a lively platform to improve their skills. We have a group of talented dancers who will hopefully bring many laurels to the college in the coming years. Quoting one of the greatest dancing legends ever, MJ
"To live is to be musical, starting with the blood dancing in your veins. Everything living has a rhythm. Do you feel your music?"
