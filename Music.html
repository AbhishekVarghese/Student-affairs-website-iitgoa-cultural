Music Club

"Music gives a soul to the universe, wings to the minds, flights to the imagination and life to everything" and IIT Goa music club truly epitomizes that. The club is studded with fantastic musicians including singers, guitarists, keyboard players etc. We have a proper soundproof music room on campus and ever-growing ensemble of musical instruments including a professional synthesizer, all types of guitars, drums, launchpad and many more.
