Photography Club

Originals IIT Goa

The photography Club of IIT Goa, aptly titled "Originals", is one of the most active clubs on campus. The club is equipped with professional cameras, camcorder, tripods, stabilizers and what not...
It captures all the golden moments and shares it with the entire IIT Goa Community. Various workshops and events are conducted to train and encourage interested students to develop their photography skills. They also cover all social platforms ranging from FB to Insta.
